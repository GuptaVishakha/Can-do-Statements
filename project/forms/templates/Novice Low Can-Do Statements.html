Novice Low Can-Do Statements

36 Points

The NCSSFL-ACTFL Can-Do Statements are self-assessment checklists used by language learners to assess what they “can do” with language in the Interpersonal, Interpretive, and Presentational modes of communication. Ultimately, the goal for all language learners is to develop a functional use of another language for one’s personal contexts and purposes. Use the checklist that will follow in a series of quizzes to record what you think you can do in the target language. You may realize that your progression may not be the same for each mode: Interpersonal, Interpretive, or Presentational. This is to be completely expected. For example, you may progress more quickly in Interpretive Reading than in Interpersonal Communication. To begin, you'll start the process with the lowest level of proficiency and work your way up from there. Make sure to be honest with yourself so you can best assess your own abilities in your target language.

Interpersonal Communication: Novice Low

I can communicate on some very familiar topics using single words and phrases that I have practiced or memorized.

Q1

I can greet my peers

Q2

I can introduce myself to someone.

Q3

I can answer a few simple questions.

Presentational Speaking: Novice Low

I can present information about myself and some other very familiar topics using single words or memorized phrases.

Q4

I can recite words and phrases I have learned.

Q5

I can state the names of familiar people, places, and objects in pictures and posters using words or memorized phrases.

Q6

I can introduce myself to a group.

Q7

I can recite short memorized phrases, parts of poems, and rhymes.

Presentational Writing: Novice Low

I can copy some familiar words, characters, or phrases.

Q8

I can copy some characters or letters and words that I see on the wall or board, in a book, or on the computer.

Q9

I can write words and phrases that I have learned.

Q10

I can label familiar people, places, and objects in pictures and posters.

Interpretive Listening: Novice Low

I can recognize a few memorized words and phrases when I hear them spoken.

Q11

I can occasionally identify the sound of a character or word.

Q12

I can occasionally understand isolated words that I have memorized, particularly when accompanied by gestures or pictures.

Interpretive Reading: Novice Low

I can recognize a few letters or characters. I can identify a few memorized words and phrases when I read.

Q13

I can recognize a few letters or characters.

Q14

I can connect some words, phrases, or characters to their meanings.
